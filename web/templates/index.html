What is this

Snitch is a Free Software server monitor. It doesn't have anything fancy, just the basics, but it does it's job, and it does it well. It's configured using a Hy DSL, which is extremely declarative, and extensible in the right places.

What it's not

This isn't a replacement for big and professional stuff like monit or nagios. This is for people who just want basic information about running services on remote boxen.
